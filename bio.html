Education

I hold a Ph.D. in Information Technologies at the University of Parma (Italy) where I also obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Engineering.

A paper describing the system designed for my final M.Sc. work was published and presented at LBS n.0 Workshop, hosted by the IEEE International Conference on Mobile Data Management (MDM).

Working Experience

I started working as an enterprise applications developer focusing on service-oriented architecture (SOA). Currently, together with my research activity – check here for details – I mostly work as a mobile Android and cross-platform developer and consultant, and as a web designer.

Some of the companies I worked for:

  • DNAPhone S.r.l. IT Consultant

    Design and developmente of B2B and B2C software systems.

  • YourAppFor IT Consultant

    Android developer and teacher.

  • CISITA Teacher

    I have been teaching the principles of cross-platform mobile programming including Python, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Java, jQuery, Design Patterns, PhoneGap and Titanium Studio.

  • Sygest IT Consultant

    Feasibility study, design and development of an HTML5, Python and OpenGL -based web application for the 3D visualization of mechanical hardware parts on mobile devices and browsers.

  • ISI IT Consultant

    Feasibility study, design and development of a mobile cross-platform (iOS and Android) sales force automation application for the online/offline management of product, customer and salesman information using Titanium Studio.

Misc

I am a member of IEEE (Computer Society and Robotics & Automation Society), INSTICC (Institute for Systems and Technologies of Information, Control and Communication) and VeRoLog (Working Group on Vehicle Routing and Logistics Optimization within EURO, the Association of the European Operational Research Societies).

SPINNER Consortium for research granted my Ph.D. activity for a project regarding collaborative mobility in smart cities.

I have been teacher assistant for the Operating Systems course at the University of Parma for B.Sc. students in Computer, Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering.

I have a professional experience with Java, C, C++, Python programming languages and with Javascript, PHP, HTML, XML, CSS web-oriented languages.

I speak English, Italian and Portuguese languages.

My Erdös number is 4 (through Katinka Wolter, who published with Miroslaw Malek, who published with Frank Harary, who published with Paul Erdös).

I am a Mendeley Advisor.
